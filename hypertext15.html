Hypertext Narrative: Clicking through Life

MAKE FOOD...

When I have the time and I don’t want to go out to eat, I cook. I try to plan out my weekly meals, but I’m not always the best at meal preparation. Today is one of those days when I don’t have anything in particular planned. I do brief inventory of the contents in my pantry and refrigerator, scroll through Pinterest to gain some inspiration, and begin cooking. Thirty minutes later, I am scarfing down food while I catch up on my Netflix shows and scroll through my various social media accounts. I am ready to go to bed.
