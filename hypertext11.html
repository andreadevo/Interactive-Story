Hypertext Narrative: Clicking through Life

RESEARCH PAPER...

For my writing class, I have a research paper due in two days about the influence of technology in the twenty-first century, specifically in education. So far, I have found some interesting articles outlining various pros and cons of the impact of technology. Personally, I see technology as a powerful tool to aid in education. However, with social media, technology also creates many distractions for students in their day-to-day lives. After working on my paper for two hours (and getting distracted by cat gifs), I debate whether I should hang out with friends or go on a run.
