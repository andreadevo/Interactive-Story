Hypertext Narrative: Clicking through Life

GO ON A RUN...

Currently, I am training to run in the Bolder Boulder in the spring. When I go running, I open my Nike running app and start my Spotify running playlist on my phone. Both of these apps significantly improve my running experience: I can easily track my time, intervals, and miles on the Nike app while also listening to my favorite workout music on Spotify. After my run, I decide whether I should work on my research paper, hang out with friends, or go out to eat.
