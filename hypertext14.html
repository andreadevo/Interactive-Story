Hypertext Narrative: Clicking through Life

GO OUT TO EAT...

I don’t always have the time (or energy) to make food, so I sometimes go out to eat. I scroll through some local Yelp! ratings before settling on a local Mexican restaurant. Using Google Maps to guide me, I quickly arrive at the restaurant. While eating, I check my social media accounts for updates. Thirty minutes later, I’ve eaten, and I’m driving home ready to go to bed.
